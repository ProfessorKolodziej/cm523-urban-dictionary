Rat-licker

A rat-licker is a person who refuses to wear a mask, or take any of the basic precautions to help society prevent an air born illness during an epidemic. (In reference to the bubonic plague being spread by rats.)

  • I went to the mall for some new underwear, but had to leave. Too many Rat-lickers.
