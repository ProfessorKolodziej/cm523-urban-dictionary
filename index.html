As per my last email

As per my last email is commonly used in professional settings to express distaste and annoyance at someone disrespecting your time by (clearly) not bothering to read the email you just sent.

Examples

  • As per my last email, I am not available this Thursday because I am already in two other meetings.
  • I am attaching the schedule again for your convenience, as per my last email.
